How To Play?

Close

  • The Quiz consists of 15 questions.
  • Each question is randomly generated and becomes more difficult as the Player progresses through them.
  • The Player is allowed 3 "Joker" buttons.
  • The Jokers are a 50-50 choice and Ask The Audience, however this Joker won't always give the correct result, as the margin of error rises with each question.
  • The Player's goal is to achieve the highest score possible.
  • Every five questions, if answered correctly, the Player gains a set highscore and can quit the game anytime.
